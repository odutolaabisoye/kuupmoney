About Kuup Money

Kuup.money is a digital cooperative platform for daily income earners that gives members unique benefits like discount purchases and access to credit as well as a way to accumulate savings and create better lives for yourself.

We supercharge a tech-enabled savings culture, which serves as the foundation for a strong financial ecosystem, from which people can obtain affordable loans and achieve financial stability.
